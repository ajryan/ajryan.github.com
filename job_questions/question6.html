If you could write a white-paper, on what subject would you focus?

I would write a white paper describing techniques for automating software localization. I have created several tools that improve the quality and productivity of localizing applications that use WPF, Windows Forms, MFC, and Crystal Reports. I would first discuss the parameters that determine quality of localization and the challenges to efficiently producing quality localizations. I would then outline general approaches to automating the localization process and measuring the quality of the localization. Finally, I would provide sample code from my automation tools with commentary on their function.
