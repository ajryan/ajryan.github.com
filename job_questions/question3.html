What do you enjoy about your current role, and what do you not enjoy?

I work side by side with internal customers of our applications. We develop two commercial application used in pharmaceutical quality control testing, and my employer uses both applications at the site where I work. I really enjoy doing “hallway usability testing” (see #12 here: The Joel Test) with the scientists who use our applications. It’s a great feeling to polish the rough edges of a prototype side-by-side with a user, and see that users are happy and productive using the new feature.

I least enjoy the politics of working for a large, bureaucratic organization. There have been several recent restructurings that have slowed down our work without perceivably affecting our productivity.
